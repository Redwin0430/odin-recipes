Turkey Protein Omlette

The turkey omlette is a staple in my protein intake of the day. Coming in at around 360 calories and 35 grams of protein, I typically ate it once a day on my weight loss journey in 2024. The omlette is fairly simple to make as it only requires a few ingredients and a little bit of time. Although its called the breakfast omlette, I typically made it the most for dinner instead of breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 95g of Egg Whites
  • Non Stick Cooking Spray
  • 60g Ground Turkey
  • Spinach
  • Gochujang Sauce
  • 28g Shredded Mozarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Season your grounded turkey with any seasoning you want. I will season with 10g of Gochujang sauce
  2. Spray your non stick cooking spray on a pan
  3. Cook your ground turkey until fully cooked
  4. Once turkey is cooked, add your spinach
  5. After 1 minute to let the spinach shrivel a bit, pour your egg whites into the pan
  6. Once your egg whites starts to bubble over, add your string cheese on top and fold your omlette in half
  7. After 1 minute on one side, flip your omlette to cook on the other side
  8. Aafter 1 minute, your omlette should be ready to serve and eat :)
