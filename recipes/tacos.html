Mexican Street Tacos

Mexican street tacos is one of the most popular street foods throughout all of Mexico. Known for being an easy and delicous snackt to prepare, the dish is often full of flavor and loved by many in both Mexico and the United States. Typically street tacos are made with a choice of meet (typically beef), cilantro, diced onions, lemon, tortillas, and red/green salsa on the side. Although, many have decided to make this dish with their own spin on the recipe. Additionally, fusions of the Mexican Street taco have been born including Taco Majal, an Indian-Mexican fusion, in NYC to taco stands being opened in Tokyo, Japan.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Olive Oil
  • Beef (or meat of choice)
  • Cilantro
  • Onion
  • Lemon
  • 3 Green Tomatillos
  • 3 Tomatoes
  • 2 cloves of Garlic
  • Jalapenos (optional)
  • Cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Dice up half an onion
  2. Bring 3 cups of water to boil
  3. Once water is boiling, add your garlic, tomatoes, and green tomatillos. Let cook for 6 min
  4. After 6 min, move your garlic, tomatoes, and tomatillos into a blender. Add a bit of water from the pot. (Can add a jalapeno to add spicyness)
  5. Blend ingredients until sauce is to your preferance (chunky or well blended)
  6. Now that the sauce is prepared, slice up your choice of meat into small bite sized peices
  7. Add olive oil on a pan and cook the choice of meat until well done
  8. After all your meat has been cooked, move your meat into a seperate container and heat up your tortillas using the SAME pan
  9. Once all tortillas have been cooked, on each tortilla serve a portion of the cooked meat, add diced oinions, cilantro, and drizzle some of the sauce (can also add shredded cheese)
