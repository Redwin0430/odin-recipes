Simple Protein Shake

The protein shake is a staple in my protein intake of the day. Coming in at around 300 calories and 44 grams of protein, I typically drink the shake once a day since I started doing calisthenics back in 2021. The protein shake is fairly simple to make as it only requires a few ingredients and a little bit of time. Should be able to make in about 5 minutes which makes it the perfect quick and filling snack.

Ingredients

  • 200 grams of milk
  • 25 grams of blueberries
  • 1 banana
  • 1 Serving of Fruity Pebble Dymatize Whey Protein
  • 85 Grams of Oikos greek yogurt

Steps

  1. In your blender, serve 200 grams of milk
  2. Then, add your 85 grams of Oikos greek yogurt
  3. Add your banana and blueberries
  4. Blend for a 1 minute
  5. Your protein shake is ready :D
